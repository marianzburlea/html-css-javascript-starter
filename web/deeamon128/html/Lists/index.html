Tips on how to travel on budget

  • Choose an afordable destination
  • Avoid tourist traps
  • Travel off-season
  • Use airbnb or hostel accomodations
  • Eat like a local
  • Use Skyscanner to find cheap flights
  • Avoid flying during the weekends
  • Contact locals that wants to practice their english so they can take you to more afordable places
  • Try to create dyi holidays. Avoid tour companies
  • Walk everywhere when it is possible

How to find cheap acommodations

  1. Use coachsurf to stay for free
  2. Airbnb
  3. Hostels
  4. Booking.com set on low to high and check for promotions
  5. On the main website of your chosen hotel. The prices could be cheaper as they won't charge for a fee for promoting

Types of afordable destinations

South East Asia

Africa

South and Central America
There are some cheap countries to travel to like Bolivia, Peru, and there are expensive countries like Mexico, Chile, Argentina.
